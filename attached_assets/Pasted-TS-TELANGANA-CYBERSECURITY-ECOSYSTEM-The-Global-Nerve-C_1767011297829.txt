THE VISIONARY FOUNDATION
A Bold Vision for Telangana's Future
Under the visionary leadership of Sri. Addanki Dayakar and Sri. Revanth Reddy, Telangana is positioned to become the undisputed global nerve center for cybersecurity. This is not merely an aspiration—it is a strategic imperative that will transform our state into the Silicon Valley of cybersecurity, creating unprecedented employment, driving GDP growth, and establishing India as a global cybersecurity superpower.

The vision is clear: Build an integrated ecosystem that delivers world-class cybersecurity talent, cutting-edge threat intelligence, and indigenous technology solutions that compete with and surpass global standards. This ecosystem will position Telangana as the go-to destination for enterprises, governments, and institutions seeking cybersecurity excellence.

1
TALENT VIA EDUCATION
Mass-scale cybersecurity education delivered through multiple channels to reach every corner of Telangana and beyond. We will create an army of cyber defenders who are job-ready from Day 1, trained on live systems with real-world threat scenarios.

Train the next generation of threat analysts and incident responders
Collaborate with global security research communities
